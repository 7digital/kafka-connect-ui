Step 2 : Decide data of interest from Twitter

This connector can track terms and/or locations and/or follow Twitter users

To track the term #Kafka use: 
    track.terms=#Kafka
To track a particular user, use tweeterid: 
    track.follow=25073877
To track a particular geo bounding box (i.e. San Francisco): 
    track.follow=-122.75,36.8,-121.75,37.8
