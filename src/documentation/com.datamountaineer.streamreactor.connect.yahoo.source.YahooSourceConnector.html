This connector allows polling either FX (Foreign Exchange) or STOCK symbols or both.
Step 1 : Decide on FX symbols to collect

To track FX rates $USD £GBP and $USD €EURO and €EURO £GBP into topic currency-rates use: 

  connect.yahoo.source.fx.subscriptions=USDGBP=X,USDEUR=X,EURGBP=X
  connect.yahoo.source.fx.topic=currency-rates
Step 2 : Decide on stock symbols to collect

To track the stock prices of GOOGLE, APPLE and MICROSOFT into topic stocks-topic use:

  connect.yahoo.source.stocks.subscriptions=GOOGL,MSFT,AAPL
  connect.yahoo.source.stocks.topic=stocks-topics
By default polls every 20 seconds the Yahoo Finance APIs.
